1. A Stage for Rising Talents and Seasoned Players

    The league promises to be a melting pot of talent, featuring a diverse array of players from the local community and beyond. It's an opportunity for rising stars to make their mark and for seasoned players to showcase their prowess. The tournament format, designed to encourage maximum participation, includes various categories ensuring inclusivity for all age groups and skill levels.

  2. The Picturesque Setting of Vilanova d'Allà

    Nestled amidst rolling hills and lush greenery, Vilanova d'Allà provides a picturesque backdrop for the league. The village's tennis facilities have been spruced up for the event, with the community coming together to ensure top-notch playing conditions. Spectators can look forward to not just thrilling matches but also the breathtaking beauty of the village.

  3. More Than Just a Tournament

    The league is more than just a series of tennis matches. It's a community event, complete with local food stalls, cultural showcases, and activities for children. The aim is to create an atmosphere of a festival, celebrating the spirit of Vilanova d'Allà and its love for tennis.
